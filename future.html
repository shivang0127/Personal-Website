Future Plans

Immediate Future

My initial plan for the coming future is to first of all complete all of my current subjects with High Distinction in each of them. After I have achieved that goal, I would want to move on to learning Data Structures and algorithms as I believe these topics are required to be applied in nearly all computer science fields which makes being proficient in them necessary, not to mention the fact that it is soon going to be one of the subjects in university as well.

Medium Term

Once I sense that I have attained an adequate amount of expertise in DSA (Data Structures and Algorithms) I would want to focus on my Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Data Analytics, and Machine Learning skills. As mentioned, my objective is to pursue a career in AI and ML. This not only roots from the fact that these are the most in-demand jobs right now or that these industries are booming, but also from the fact that I have some deep interest in Mathematics, and these fields being Math-intensive further reinstates my interest in them.
This is not going to be the easiest task to tackle as the topics mentioned above are extremely rigorous and require thorough understanding of their contents before being able to apply them. This would involve diligent study at university along with completing online courses to reinstitute the learning.

Along with every goal mentioned above, I would also like to work on programming projects that are separate from university. This would involve investing time on learning new topics and applying them to create projects that can be shared on my portfolio.

Long Term

My next goal is to acquire an internship and work as an intern at a reputed firm to gain experience and become job-ready. Internships are somewhat hard to land, especially for someone like me who have no real-world experience as a programmer, but I will try to obtain knowledge on how a student like myself can secure an internship by communicating with seniors who have already worked as interns, along with alumni who are currently working full-time jobs. I will also try to have a good-enough portfolio of personal projects before applying for internships.

Personal Goals

The first personal goal I have in my mind that I want to work on is time management. As of now, I primarily let my university schedule dictate how my days go. This means that when I do not have any classes, those days go without any specific schedule. This turns to a lack of discipline and less productivity compared to how much I should achieve. Thus, I want to have a specific schedule that divides my time into different blocks during the day to improve productivity.

I also want to work on my soft skills including leadership, communication, and teamworking skills to be an overall better performer not only in university but also in the workforce. This will be achieved by participating more actively in tutorials or labs to build confidence in communication, and being more vocal and active in the daily life.

Lastly, I would also love to work on open-source projects to learn about different dynamics that come into play when working on programming projects with multiple people. This would not only improve my networking/connections, but also broaden my coding skills.
